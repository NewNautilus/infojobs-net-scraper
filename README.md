## What does InfoJobs Scraper do?

InfoJobs Scraper is a tool that allows you to extract job postings data from www.infojobs.net/ quickly and easily. It extracts data such as:

- Company name
- Job title
- Location
- Salary
- Contract type
- Job description

## How can you use the InfoJobs data?

The InfoJobs data you can extract with this scraper can be used for a variety of purposes, such as:

- **Job market analysis**: Analyze trends in job postings, such as the most in-demand skills or the average salary for specific positions.
- **Competitive intelligence**: Monitor job postings by competitors to gain insights into their hiring practices and strategies.
- **Research**: Use the data for academic or industry research on employment trends, skills demand, or labor market dynamics.
- **Career planning**: Identify opportunities for reskilling or upskilling based on in-demand positions and required qualifications.
- **Data visualization**: Create maps or graphs showing job distribution across regions or sectors, helping to identify hotspots of opportunity or under-employment.

## How to scrape InfoJobs

It is super easy to scrape www.infojobs.net/ with InfoJobs Scraper. Just follow these few steps and you will get your data in a few minutes.

1. Click on **Try for free**.
2. Enter the search URL with filter options or location for your search.
3. Click on **Save & Start**.
4. When the scraper has finished, you can preview and download your data from the Output tab.
